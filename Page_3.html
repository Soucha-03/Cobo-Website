1. Monk Maz Koshia


Monk Maz Koshia is the final boss of the
Champion’s Ballad DLC for Breath of the
Wild. You can find him after completing all
the missions and clearing the dungeon. Be
careful, as he is by far the hardest boss in
the game.

This fight is made difficult by the amount of
health he has, and the variety of his attacks.
Even if he doesn’t hit as hard as a Lynel, he still
makes you lose a lot in no time, so make sure
you have a lot of food. The battle is well worth
it though, as if you win, you get the rune that
summon the fabled Master Cycle Zero.
