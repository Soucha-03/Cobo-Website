2. Lynels


Even though Lynels technically aren’t bosses,
they are still one of the strongest opponents
you could stumble upon. You can find them in
multiple areas, but the first lynel you will
encounter is during the Vah Ruta Quest.

If you ever find one, make sure he doesn’t
notice you, as if he does, he will shoot arrows
at you with deadly aim. And if you decide to
fight, make sure you have good armor, as he
hits very hard.
